Graphite

Graphite on paper is the beat medium for sahdow play or playing with light. It is a part of monochrome division of art. It by far requires the least amount of materials, also it's materials are easily avaliable.

Charcoal

Charcoal on paper is one of the oldest form of sketching arts. It is very similar to graphite on paper with an additional benigit of darker darks. It provides more deapth than graphite.

Ink

Ink on paper is one of those art forms that requires precision and has very less scope for mistakes. It is more defined and sharp featured. Mostly applicable in doodling and subject based drawings.
